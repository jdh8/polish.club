# Inviting 2M

Bidding after 2M implies invitational+ since 2M is already an appealing
contract.

| 1♣-1M 2M- | MIN, 11--14, 4=M |
|-----------|------------------|
| 2NT!      | INV+, no longer side suits
| 3♣♦       | INV, 5+#
| 3M!       | LIM, (4333)
| 3NT       | COG, (4333)
| 4♣♦!      | S/T SPL, 0--1#

As the highest step of invitation, 3M should be very specific.  I choose (4333)
to enable choice of games between 3NT and 4M.

Why bother bidding another suit to 2M?  It helps finding double fits.

| 1♣-1♥ 2♥- | MIN, 11--14, 4=♥ |
|-----------|------------------|
| 2♠        | INV, 4+♠, 5+♥
| 3♠!       | S/T SPL, 0--1#

| 1♣-1♠ 2♠- | MIN, 11--14, 4=♠ |
|-----------|------------------|
| 3♥        | INV, 4+♥, 5+♠
| 4♥!       | S/T SPL, 0--1#
