# Polish 1♣

This Polish Club has 3 variants:

- 12--14, 2--4♠, 2--4♥, 2--4♦, 2--4♣
- 11--17, 5+♣ or 4414
- 18+, not suitable for 1♦

Note that 2♣ is no longer natural since *Polish Club 2020: Expert*.  1♣ takes
care of all ♣-suited hands.

I move diamond invitation down to 1♣-2♠ in case of a misfit.  Strong balanced
hands go to 1♣-3♦ and 1♣-1♦.

I also swap 1♣-2♣♦ because the potential club fit saves bidding space.  On the
other hand, the diamond suit requires more space to explore a fit.
