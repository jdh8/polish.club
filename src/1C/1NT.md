# Positive 1NT, 2NT

I group these suitless positive responses together for simplicity. These
responses deny 4-card majors and 6-card minors.

| 1♣- | Strength | Distribution |
|-----|----------|--------------|
| 1NT |  8--10   | 2--3♠, 2--3♥, 2--5♦, 2--5♣
| 2NT | 10--11   | 2--3♠, 2--3♥, 2--5♦, 2--5♣
