# (1Y)2Y

## Michaels over minor suits

Subsequent bidding is mostly natural since there are already three suits on the
table.

| (1♣)2♣ | Michaels, 5+♠, 5+♥ |
|--------|--------------------|
| 2♦     | NF, usually 6+♦
| 2♥♠    | NAT, 1+#
| 2NT    | NAT INV
| 3♣!    | INV+, usually 3+♠♥
| 3♦     | FG, usually 6+♦
| 3♥♠    | PRE, 3+#

| (1♦)2♦ | Michaels, 5+♠, 5+♥ |
|--------|--------------------|
| 2♥♠    | NAT, 1+#
| 2NT    | NAT INV
| 3♣     | NF, usually 6+♣
| 3♦!    | INV+, usually 3+♠♥
| 3♥♠    | PRE, 3+#
