# Odwrotka 2♦

Odwrotka is an artificial game-forcing raise of 3+ card support.
I propose two sets of responses to Odwrotka.

## Reverse 445566

The simple 445566 structure is popular in Poland.  I revert the strong and weak
steps to prevent the weak hand from declaring notrump via bidding 2NT.

| 1♣-1M-2♦- | FG, 3+ SUPP |
|-----------|-------------|
| 2♥!       | S/T, 10+, 4=M
| 2♠!       | MIN, 7--10, 4=M
| 2NT!      | S/T, 10+, 5=M
| 3♣!       | MIN, 7--10, 5=M
| 3♦!       | S/T, 10+, 6+M
| 3M        | MIN, 7--9, 6+M

## Strong 2♥ {#Strong-2H}

I recommend the relay structure in *Polish Club 2020: Expert*.  The 2♥ response
to Odwrotka is the strongest step combining all slam tries.  The relay structure
generally lets the strong opener declare notrump.

| 1♣-1M-2♦- | FG, 3+ SUPP |
|-----------|-------------|
| 2♥!       | S/T, 10+
| 2♠!       | NEG, 7--10, 4=M
| 2NT!      | MIN, 7--10, 5=M, not BAL
| 3♣        | Mild S/T, 9--11, 5+♣, 4=M
| 3♦        | Mild S/T, 9--11, 5+♦, 4=M
| 3M!       | MIN, 7--10, BAL 5=M
| 3OM!      | SPL, 7--9, 0--1#, 6+M
| 3NT       | MIN, 7--9, 6+M
| 4♣!       | SPL, 7--9, 0--1♣, 6+M
| 4♦!       | SPL, 7--9, 0--1♦, 6+M

| ...-2♥- | S/T, 10+ |
|---------|----------|
| 2♠!     | (R), 3--5M
| 2NT     | NAT, 3=M
| 3♣♦     | S/T, 5+#
| 3M      | NAT S/T, 4+#
| 3NT!    | BAL COG, 18--20, 4M333

| ...-2♥-2♠- | (R) |
|------------|-----|
| 2NT        | 4=M
| 3♣         | 4+♣, 5=M
| 3♦         | 4+♦, 5=M
| 3OM        | 4+#, 5=M
| 3M!        | BAL 5=M
| 3NT!       | 6+M
| 4♣!        | SPL, 0--1♣, 6+M
| 4♦!        | SPL, 0--1♦, 6+M

| ...-2♥-2♠-2NT- | 4=M |
|----------------|-----|
| 3♣!            | Ask for a side suit
| 3♣-3X          | 4+#
| 3♣-3M!         | 4+♣
| 3♣-3NT         | 4M(333)

### Extension to 2♥

I designed rebids other than the 2♠ relay for more descriptive auctions.

| ...-2♥-2NT- | 3=M |
|-------------|-----|
| 3♣          | 4+♣, 5+M
| 3♦          | 4+♦, 5+M
| 3OM         | 4+#, 5+M
| 3M          | 5+M w/o side suit
| 3NT         | 4=M
