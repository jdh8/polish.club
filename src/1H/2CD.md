# Game-forcing 2♣♦

Unpassed 2/1 is 100% game-forcing.  Opener defaults to the lowest rebid.
Opener's reverses promise extra strength.

| 1♥-2♣  | NAT FG |
|--------|--------|
| 2♦!    | 4+♦ or BAL MIN
| 2♥     | MIN, not BAL
| 2♠     | MAX, 4+♠
| 2NT    | BAL MAX
| 3♣     | MAX, 4+♣
| 3♦     | MAX, 5+♦
| 3♥     | MAX, 6+♥
| 3♠     | MIN, 5+♠, 6+♥
| 3NT    | MIN, 5=♥, 1--2♣
