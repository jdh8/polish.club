# BTU Stayman 2♣

BTU Stayman is characterized by including invitations of 5= spades.  All its
variants are:

- **Weak:** T/O with short clubs (Garbage Stayman)
- **Invitational:** 4--**5**♠ (BTU) or 4=♥
- **Game-forcing:** 4=♠ or 4=♥

| 1NT-2♣- | BTU STAY |
|---------|----------|
| 2♦!     | 2--3♠, 2--3♥
| 2♥      | 4--5♥, 2--4♠
| 2♠      | 4--5♠, 2--3♥

## 1NT-2♣-2♦ {#1NT-2C-2D}

- Second responses above 3NT remain the same, assuming misfit.
