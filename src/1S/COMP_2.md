# Competitive bidding

Notrump becomes cuebid after a double.  We still need fit-showing jumps, so
Truscott 2NT fits well.

| 1♠(X) | - |
|-------|---|
| XX    | NF BAL G/T, 10+
| 1♠    | Usually 5+♠
| 1NT!  | TRF, 5+♣
| 2♣!   | TRF, 5+♦
| 2♦!   | TRF, 5+♥
| 2♥!   | TRF, 8+, 3+♠
| 2♠    | PRE, 0--7, 3+♠
| 2NT!  | LIM+, 4+♠
| 3♣♦!  | CONST+, 5+#, 4+♠
| 3♥!   | INV, 6+♥, 3=♠
| 3♠    | COMP, 7--9, 4+♠

Natural defense suffices over 1♠(1NT).

| 1♠(1NT) | - |
|---------|---|
| X       | PEN, INV+
| 2NT!    | LIM 4+♠ or UNBAL FG

Transfers à la Rubens applies over 1♠(2♣♦♥).  Note that 2NT is natural because
we still have 2♠.

| 1♠(2X) | - |
|--------|---|
| X      | T/O
| 2Y     | NF, 5+#
| 2♠     | NF, 3+♠
| 2NT    | NAT, 9--11
| 3♥!    | LIM+, 3+♠
| 3♠     | CONST, 4+♠

| 1♠(2♣) | - |
|--------|---|
| 3♣!    | INV+, 6+♦
