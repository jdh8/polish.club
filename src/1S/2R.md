# Game-forcing 2♦♥

Unpassed 2/1 is 100% game-forcing.  Opener defaults to the lowest rebid.
Opener's reverses promise extra strength.

> [!IMPORTANT]
>
> 1♠-2♦-2♥♠ swap their natural meanings for several reasons:
>
> - The lowest rebid of 2♥ improves precision of 6-2 COG.
> - As Ekren already takes invitational 4=♥, the range of 4=♥ is narrow.
