# Maggie 2♣

Unlike 1♥-1NT, 1♠-1NT may conceal a heart fit.  Finding a 3-5 fit for 4♥ after
1♠-1NT is pretty challenging.  [Bart] is a convention after forcing 1NT that
tries to handle heart invitations.  [Lisa], Bart's smarter sister, is an
improvement on Bart.  Maggie, the youngest Simpson, solves this problem at
1♠-2♣ instead.

[Bart]: https://www.larryco.com/bridge-articles/bart
[Lisa]: https://www.bridgewebs.com/ocala/Lisa%20Bidding%20Convention.pdf

In a natural 2/1 bidding system, 1♠-2♣-2♦♥ are underused.  These steps are a
better place for a convention than inside the overloaded 1♠-1NT.

| 1♠-2♣- | NAT FG or INV 5--6♥ |
|--------|---------------------|
| 2♦!    | (R), 0--4♥
| 2♥!    | MIN, 11--13, 2--3♥
| 2♠     | MIN, 11--13, 6+♠, 0--2♥
| 2NT!   | MAX, 6+♠, 0--2♥
| 3♣     | MAX, 4+♣
| 3♦     | MAX, 5+♦
| 3♥     | MAX, 5+♥
| 3♠!    | MAX, 6+♠, 3=♥
| 3NT!   | MIN, 5+♥
| 4♣!    | MIN FRAG, 0--1♦, 3+♥, 4+♣
| 4♦!    | MAX SPL, 0--1♦, 3+♥, 4+♣

- **2♥:** To play in hearts with an invitational hand.
- **2♦:** Not to play 2♥ with an invitational hand:
  - Any game force
  - 12--13 that want to sign off at the 2-level

| 1♠-2♣-2♦- | (R) |
|-----------|-----|
| 2♥        | NF, 6=♥ or 0--1♠
| 2♠        | NF, 2+♠
| 2NT+      | NAT FG

| 1♠-2♣-2♥- | MIN 2--3♥ |
|-----------|-----------|
| 2♠        | NF, 3=♠
| 2NT+      | NAT FG

Compared with the default 1♠-2♣-2♠ in 2/1, Maggie occupies no more space.
Non-forcing sequences use space only up to 2♠.
